We're so glad you're interested in Playground City! Here's some ways you can get involved and stay updated.

## Join the Community

You can hear news and discuss the game on [Discord](https://outside-promo-site.netlify.app/playtest/#) or [Reddit.](https://outside-promo-site.netlify.app/playtest/#)

You can also sign up to our [email list](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdhGU8CJW1lhq6K8SrxCezvmSDdzIZUXaCPrNk34bIWliY2vQ/viewform) for regular updates.

## Become a playtester

You can help improve the game (and play before release) as a playtester. [Sign up here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdhGU8CJW1lhq6K8SrxCezvmSDdzIZUXaCPrNk34bIWliY2vQ/viewform).
