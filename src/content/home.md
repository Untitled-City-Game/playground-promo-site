## Transit and tactics

Playground City is played in person, using real-world public transport.

You'll navigate strategically around the city, anticipating your opponent's moves.

## Explore your town with friends

Playground City is played with four to twelve people, over an afternoon or full day.

The game will take you and your friends all over your town, giving you a reason to see new and interesting places.

> I knew about this area, but I never had a reason to go. Now when I visit I'll remember this game!
> 
> \- _Mark, playtester_

## Many game modes

This game is really many games! We're creating many [game modes](/games), all with the central idea of adapting a classic, simple game to be played over a city's transit network.
